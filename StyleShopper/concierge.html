REDISCOVER YOUR STYLE

Pair complementing colours - Select outfits in an instant - Shop with confidence

Do you feel uninspired when it comes to choosing your outfits? It’s a common problem for many of the lovely women I work with. But knowledge is power - once you know which colours suit you best and most complement your body shape, it will make your life so much easier.

In a Colour & Body Shape Rediscovery session, I’ll explain colour analysis, so you know exactly which shades will illuminate your skin tone. We’ll discuss the best styles for your body shape, as well as the reality of how much time and energy you have to put into picking outfits. I’ll give you clear and simple-to-apply advice that will enable you to select clothes which give you confidence, reflect the true you and suit your busy mum life, in an instant.
