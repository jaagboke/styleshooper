Wardrobe Edit

Your personal needs are determined through an initial telephone conversation, we’ll go through your wardrobe and I will advice on outfits that flatter your body type and and consider outfits that deserve to stay in your wardrobe. You will also try on different combinations of outfits from your wardrobe. After which we will identify what’s missing and suggest new styles that will work with your existing pieces. I will also advice on where you can shop for new pieces.

Please note that 3 hours is the minimum time to complete the Wardrobe Edit service. Timing is difficult to predict as everyone’s wardrobe is unique. Any service that exceeds 2 hours will incur the maximum 3 hour fee.


Personal Shopping

I will take the stress off you by making your shopping experience fun and productive. My first consultation with you will be to identify what to need, what you currently have in your wardrobe and discuss pieces you need to make your wardrobe functional for your everyday dressing. After which I compile a shopping list for you and go pre-shopping at the stores and online to ensure items availability so that our shopping session is efficient and focused.

I select a range of clothes that will suit your body shape, lifestyle needs and budget. From your new pieces I will give advice on how to style them beautifully and look well put together. And you will be leaving the personal shopping session with outfits you’ll love that’s exactly suited to your lifestyle, you will also be given handy tips for the next time you go shopping on your own.


Outfit Build

Here, i will create outfits from head to toe using the pieces you already own, including your clothes, accessories, shoes and handbags all based on your professional and social commitments. Each outfit is then screen shot and put together in a ‘look book’ and sent to you via email.

It may be perfect idea to stick it onto the back of your wardrobe door or saving on your phone, so that whenever you find yourself saying, ‘I have nothing to wear,’ you’ll have the look book to guide you.

One outfit can be styled several ways, and my pricing is based on ways in which I am able to build different styles with that one outfit. With max of 5 ways per outfit.


Concierge Service

This mainly deals with ordering items for you, when you already have links or pictures of your preferred items.


Maternity Styling

If you are pregnant or a nursing mom and you are basically looking for ways not to loose your sense of style, this service is specifically for you. I will give advice on how to style yourself including where to shop for flattering outfits even for your upcoming occasions.
